Purpose

The purpose of my website is to establish an online presence for our rafting business, providing a platform to showcase our services, attract new customers, and facilitate bookings for rafting adventures.

Audience

The website target audience consists of adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and outdoor enthusiasts seeking memorable experiences in nature. They are typically between the ages of 18 to 40, with moderate to high disposable income, and have a passion for outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and water sports.

Color Palette

For our rafting site, we've opted for a color palette that reflects the serene beauty of nature and the adventurous spirit of rafting.

  • Primary Color: #DAD7CD (Misty Gray) - This soft and neutral gray evokes the misty mornings on the river, setting a calm and tranquil tone for the site.
  • Secondary Color: #A3B18A (Sage Green) - Inspired by the lush greenery surrounding the riverbanks, this earthy green brings a sense of vitality and freshness to the design.
  • Accent 1 Color: #4A6670 (River Blue) - Reflecting the shimmering hues of the water, this deep blue color adds depth and richness to the palette, reminding visitors of the adventure that awaits.
  • Accent 2 Color: #8A3033 (Adventure Red) - Symbolizing the thrill and excitement of rafting, this bold red hue injects energy and passion into the design, capturing the adventurous spirit of our expeditions.
